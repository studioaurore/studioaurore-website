gloria & georgio

In crafting the branding for event photographers Gloria & Georgio, our aim was to encapsulate the essence of their dynamic and adventurous spirit while maintaining a sense of tranquility and elegance. Inspired by the vast landscapes of the outback and the joyous celebrations of weddings and parties, we curated a visual identity that harmonizes calming pastel tones with dynamic illustrations. Each element was carefully crafted to reflect their passion for storytelling through images. The result is a visual identity that mirrors Gloria & Georgio's unique style and expertise, inviting clients to embark on a journey of unforgettable moments captured in time.
