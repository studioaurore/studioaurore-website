our design process

Upon receiving your enquiry, I will reach out to begin discussing the details of your project. You'll receive a comprehensive proposal and contract. Once approved, a deposit will secure your spot in my schedule.

Now for the exciting part! I'll transform the insights gathered during the Discovery phase into a design that authentically reflects your brand's values and goals. Get ready to witness your vision come to life!

Once you've approved your new branding, I'll swiftly prepare the files and hand them over to you. It's time to proudly showcase your stunning new design to the world

You'll complete a brand questionnaire to provide insights into your business and goals. This step ensures that we're aligned with your vision before proceeding further with the project.

Now we focus on fine-tuning and perfecting the design to ensure it meets your expectations and vision. This is your opportunity to provide feedback and revisions, ensuring the final product aligns seamlessly with your brand identity and goals.
